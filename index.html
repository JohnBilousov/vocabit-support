Frequently asked questions

What is Vocabit?

Vocabit helps you learn vocabulary with flashcards. Browse categories or study sets, then practice with Flashcards, Learn mode, Tests, and the Daily Challenge.

What's included in Vocabit Pro?

Vocabit Pro unlocks unlimited study sets, Learn mode with spaced repetition, offline access (download sets), advanced test options (Written & True/False), and premium voice audio.

How do I subscribe to Vocabit Pro?

Open the app, tap “Unlock Pro,” and choose a plan. The subscription is an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple App Store account.

How do I restore a purchase?

On the “Unlock Pro” screen, tap “Restore Purchase.” Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your device, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, select Vocabit, and cancel or change your plan. Cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

Can I use Vocabit offline?

Yes — with Vocabit Pro you can download study sets and review them without an internet connection.

Is my data safe?

We take your privacy seriously. See our Privacy Policy for details on what we collect and how it's used.

I found a bug or have a feature request

Please email ultimate.solutions@icloud.com with a short description (and a screenshot if possible). We read every message.
